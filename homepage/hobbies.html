Ju-Jutsu is a german martial art for self defense. It is a hybrid martial art that incorporates elements from traditional Japanese Ju-Jutsu, Judo, and other martial arts. German Ju-Jutsu places a strong emphasis on practical self-defense techniques, and it covers a wide range of techniques, including strikes, throws, joint locks, and ground fighting.
The style is often tailored for real-world self-defense situations and includes techniques for defending against various types of attacks. German Ju-Jutsu is practiced by both civilians and law enforcement personnel, and it has gained popularity in Germany and other parts of Europe.
